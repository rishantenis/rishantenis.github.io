Topic Modelling

Extracted abstract topic of a text using LDA(Latent Dirichlet Allocation). Cleaned text broadly including removal of URl, mentions and hastags; expansion of contractions; reducing to base form and removal of accents. Determined Resonable no of topic using Coherence Score.
